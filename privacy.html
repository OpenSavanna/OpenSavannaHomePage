Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Introduction

Open Savanna ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number when you book a call or contact us
  • Business Information: Company name, role, project details shared during consultations
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through secure third-party payment providers (M-Pesa, bank transfers)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referring website
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our consulting services
  • Communicate with you about projects and appointments
  • Send relevant updates about our services (with your consent)
  • Process payments and maintain records
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Cal.com (scheduling), payment processors, email services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent for project collaboration

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Cal.com). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
