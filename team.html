The People Behind Open Savanna

A small, senior team combining world-class engineering with deep local context to serve organizations across East Africa.

Open Savanna pairs international engineering standards, the practices proven in the world's most mature tech markets, with deep local market understanding to help East African organizations scale. We keep the team deliberately lean and hands-on, so the people who scope your work are the people who deliver it, backed by a vetted network of specialists when a project needs more hands.
