Project work is milestone-based, so you only pay for completed, approved work. Every engagement starts with a free discovery call, and for ongoing support we offer a first-month money-back guarantee.

Common Questions

How do payments work?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and card. Project work is milestone-based, and ongoing support is billed monthly.

How is pricing set?

Most services are fixed-scope and fixed-price, quoted after a short discovery call so you know the cost upfront. Listed prices are starting points that depend on size and scope.

Do you handle tenders & custom projects?

Yes. For government and NGO tenders and larger bespoke work, we scope and quote per project. Book a call to discuss.

Can you work within our budget?

Often, yes. Tell us your budget on the call and we'll propose a scope that fits, or tell you honestly if it isn't feasible.
