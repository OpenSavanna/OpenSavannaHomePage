Common Questions

How do payments work?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and card payments. Monthly packages are billed at the start of each month. Project work can be milestone-based.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly packages have no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime before your next billing cycle.

What if I need more hours?

Additional hours can be purchased at KES 3,000/hour for Grow tier and KES 4,000/hour for Scale tier.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes. Pay annually and get 2 months free (equivalent to ~17% discount).
