Our Work

Case Study

Education / Digital Transformation

Digitizing Records for Kabarnet School for the Deaf and Blind

The Challenge

Kabarnet School for the Deaf and Blind in Baringo County was managing all student records, staff information, and administrative data through paper-based systems. This created inefficiencies, risk of data loss, and made reporting to the Ministry of Education time-consuming.

Our Solution

We implemented a locally-hosted records management system using Microsoft products, enabling the school to digitize student enrollment records, track academic progress, and streamline administrative workflows. The system was designed to work reliably with limited internet connectivity.

Results

  • Reduced paperwork and manual record-keeping by over 60%
  • Faster retrieval of student information for reporting
  • Improved data security with digital backups
  • Staff trained on system use for long-term sustainability
"The system has transformed how we manage our records. What used to take hours of searching through files now takes minutes. Our staff can focus more on the students instead of paperwork."
Salina Binot Former Principal, Kabarnet School for the Deaf and Blind
